EMURZ

AI based interactive interface for interactive games .

Welcome to the future of gaming! We are proud to introduce our groundbreaking Deep-Learning based gaming interface for Virtual Reality (VR) and Extended Reality (XR). Harnessing the power of cutting-edge technology, we have created a seamless and immersive gaming experience like no other. At the heart of our innovation lies Mediapipe, a state-of-the-art human landmark points tracking system. By leveraging the capabilities of Deep Learning, we have developed a highly accurate and real-time tracking system that captures the precise movements of the user in the virtual environment. This technology ensures that your every action and gesture is mirrored within the game, creating a truly immersive and interactive gaming experience. To bring this vision to life, we have utilized the Unity engine, a powerful and versatile platform for game development. Our skilled team of developers has created a custom-made virtual environment that serves as the canvas for your gaming adventures. From breathtaking landscapes to thrilling action sequences, our virtual environment will transport you to a world beyond imagination. We are proud to announce that our innovation has garnered recognition from META (formerly known as Facebook). We have been selected to participate in the prestigious META XR program, a testament to the quality and potential of our gaming interface. This program provides us with unique opportunities for collaboration, support, and access to cutting-edge resources, enabling us to continually push the boundaries of VR and XR gaming. With our Deep-Learning based gaming interface, you can embark on incredible virtual journeys, conquer challenging quests, and engage in exhilarating multiplayer battles. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to the world of VR and XR, our interface offers a user-friendly and intuitive experience that will keep you hooked for hours on end.
